Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2, 2026 | Last Updated: June 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Semiconk respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all of our users. This includes:

  • Account information: Personal markers such as name, email address, password hash details, and active telephone numbers.
  • Location data: Coordinates and proximity flags, collected only with your explicit permission, used solely to compute distance maps to nearby suppliers.
  • Company information: Registered corporate handles, operational addresses, inventory counts, product categories, and website links for registered suppliers.
  • Usage data: Analytical records tracking query parameters, page hits, interactive clicks, and preferred categories.
  • Device information: Technical diagnostics detailing browser types, general operating system configurations, localized IP ranges, and device signatures.

3. How We Use Your Information

The stored information operates as functional telemetry for the following utilities:

  • To provide and improve our services.
  • To connect active manufacturing builders with verified localized suppliers and warehousing facilities.
  • To send relevant updates and voluntary opt-in promotional materials.
  • To prevent fraud, verify operational business licenses, and maintain platform security.
  • To comply with regulatory subpoenas, active judicial mandates, and generic legal compliance.

4. Data Protection

Your data is encrypted and stored securely using state-of-the-art secure network practices. We do not sell your personal identification records or private accounts to third-party brokers. Users maintain absolute rights in accordance with international frameworks to inspect, edit, export, or permanently erase their stored profiles or credentials at any moment.

5. Location Data

Location information acts solely as transient or stored parameters to evaluate 40km proximity radii of components. It is never exposed publicly on the map or sold. You can switch off location tracking or reset your primary coordinates at any time via your browser settings or platform profiles.

6. Children's Privacy

Semiconk is not intended for and goes unoffered to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly compile or retain accounts for students or minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect operational changes or external compliance frameworks. Significant adjustments will trigger a platform notice or direct email announcement.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions, disputes, or recommendations touching your data protection, please contact our compliance desk at: privacy@semiconk.com
